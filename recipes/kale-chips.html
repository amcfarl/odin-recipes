Kale Chips with Honey

Description

Get your daily dose of greens in with the sweet but crispy kale snack

Ingredients

  • 2 bunches dino kale
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Steps

  1. Combine kale, olive oil, lemon juice, and sea salt in resealable bag
  2. Massage mixture for 3 minutes
  3. Drizzle honey over mixture and massage gently for about 3 minutes
  4. Cook at 115 degrees F in food dehydrator for 8 hours
