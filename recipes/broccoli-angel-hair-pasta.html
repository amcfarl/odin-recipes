Broccoli Garlic Angel Hair Pasta

Description

Don't like broccoli? Try it with Angel Hair Pasta! This mix of pasta, broccoli, and garlic is savory and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds broccoli
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 6 gloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 3 cups chicken stock
  • salt to taste
  • 1 pound angel hair pasta
  • 1/2 cup shredded Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Cut the florets from the broccoli stems into a bowl.
  2. Chop remaining stems into small pieces and transfer stems to separate bowl
  3. Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil with butter in skillet over medium heat and cook garlie in mixture
  4. Stir brocolli stems into stock mixture and simmer 10-12 minutes
  5. Stir brocolli florets into skillet and cook brocolli sauce 2-4 minutes
  6. Cook angel hair pasta, about 4 minutes
  7. Pour broccoli sauce over pasta and mix in cheese
